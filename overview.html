Website Project

After finishing with the learning part of our homework, which included courses in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript on the codacademy.com site we pursued the creation of our team site. The backbone was presented by Babek, our team leader, who accurately wrote the HTML code for the structuring. The context, which will be displayed on our site, was written by Tamella. Description of the process and general information and background of our team and the general information about each member including the photos was located in the hyperlink that is located on the home page of our site. The process of homework two, which was about the "Hour of code", is displayed on the home page of the site. To conclude, this experience helped us to understand the structure of the site as well as the importance of the design that helps attract the user and increases the quality of interaction.
